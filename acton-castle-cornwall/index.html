John Wood the Younger Buildings

Acton Castle, Cornwall

Architect: John Wood the Younger

Date: 1773

Description:

Acton Castle over looking St Michael’s Mount in Cornwall was built in 1773. It was designed by John Wood the Younger and constructed in brick faced with local Cornish granite, for John Stackhouse who was a marine biologist. 

It now split into apartments with five of them being available as holiday lets. For more information actoncastle.com.
