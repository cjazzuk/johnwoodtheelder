National Trust - ‘Georgian Experience’ Bath Assembly Rooms

3th July 2022

March 2023 sees the National Trust taking over the Bath Assembly Rooms. They have owned them since 1931 but have leased it to the North East Somerset Council who have housed the Fashion Museum their.

See the update from The National Trust and their plan for Bath Assembly Rooms.

The Bath Assembly Rooms were designed by John Wood the Younger 1769-1771
