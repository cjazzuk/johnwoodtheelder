Royal Mineral Water Hospital, Bath

Architect: John Wood the Elder

Date: 1738-42

Description:

The Royal Mineral Water Hospital was founded in 1738. It provided care for the impoverished sick who were attracted to Bath because of the supposed healing properties of the mineral water. The original building was designed by John Wood the Elder and built with Bath stone donated by Ralph Allen and was completed in 1742.

The attic storey was added later in 1793.
