Ground Radar John Wood’s Ledger Stone

14th November 2022

Last week Bristol University visited St Mary’s Church at Upper Swainswick, the final resting place of John Wood the Elder and his family members.

They carried out ground radar analysis above the ledger stones of John Wood the Elder and one of his daughters to try and find out whether there is a family vault below and whether their are more people interred beneath.
