St. Mary's Chapel, Queen Square, Bath

Architect: John Wood the Elder

Date: 1734

Description:

Part of John Wood the Elder’s plan for Queen Square was to include a Chapel for the residents. St. Mary’s Chapel stood at the south-west of Queen Square in Chapel Row. The foundation stone of the chapel was laid on 25th March 25th 1732 by John Wood the Elder. It was opened on 25th December 1734.

It was demolished in 1875 to enable Chapel Row to be widened and improve the access to Green Park Station.
