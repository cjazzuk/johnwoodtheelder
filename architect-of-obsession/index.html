Architect of Obsession

2nd May 2022

May 2022 will see the newly illustrated and revised edition of ‘Architect of Obsession : John Wood and the Creation Bath’ by Timothy Mowl & Brian Earnshaw with Cathryn Spence. For anyone interested in more details on the life and history of John Wood this is a must have.

My copy of the original is well worn and I cannot wait for the new edition. Its 304 pages have been beautifully updated with new photos and colour illustrations.
